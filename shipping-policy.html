SHIPPING
POLICY

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

1. Order Processing Time

All orders are processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

2. Delivery Estimates

  • Inside Kathmandu Valley: 1-3 business days
  • Outside Kathmandu Valley: 3-7 business days depending on the region

Please note that rural or remote destinations may take slightly longer. Delays occasionally occur due to high volume, weather, or local logistics challenges.

3. Shipping Rates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. Rates are determined based on the total weight of your order and your exact delivery location within Nepal.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, we will send you an email or SMS notification containing delivery details and tracking information if available from our logistics partners.
